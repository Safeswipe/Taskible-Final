Frequently Asked Questions

What software do your VAs use?

Our bookkeepers are trained in Xero and QuickBooks, and we match you with one who fits your system.

Do I need to train the VA?

Nope — we handle all onboarding. Just send us your existing processes (if any), and your VA will be ready to go.

Can I talk to my VA directly?

Yes. You’ll be connected via Slack and Trello for smooth, direct communication.

Is it safe to give a VA access to my financial data?

Absolutely. All VAs sign strict confidentiality agreements, and we recommend using read-only logins or secure tools like Xero’s advisor invites.

What if I want to switch VAs?

You can switch anytime. We guarantee 100% satisfaction and flexibility as your business grows.
